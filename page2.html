Foundation Drawing

Foundation drawing is essential because it builds the core skills of observation, proportion, and understanding form. By learning how to draw from life, artists develop visual awareness and control that support all other practices, from illustration and animation to painting and design.

It provides the structure behind creative expression and helps ideas feel intentional and grounded.
