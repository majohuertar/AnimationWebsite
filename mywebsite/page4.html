Digital Programs

These resources focus on digital art programs, tools, and plugins that can support drawing, painting, and animation workflows. They include both free and paid software options for different types of artists and devices.

Learning digital programs is important because they help artists explore different workflows, practice more efficiently, and build technical skills that support both personal and professional projects.
