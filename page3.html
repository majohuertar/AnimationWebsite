Color

These resources focus on color theory, palette building, skin tones, mixing, and digital tools that help artists understand how color works in both design and illustration.

Learning color is important because it shapes mood, clarity, contrast, and visual meaning. A strong understanding of color helps artists make better choices in composition, lighting, and storytelling.
